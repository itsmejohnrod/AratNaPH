Full Package Description

This tour emphasizes the natural landscapes surrounding Baguio. You'll spend time in the scenic pine-filled environment of Camp John Hay, enjoy a relaxing boat ride on Burnham Park lake, and get hands-on experience picking fresh produce at the famous La Trinidad Strawberry Farm.

Perfect for outdoor lovers and those seeking a cool, refreshing getaway. The package includes transportation and cozy accommodation away from the city bustle.

Itinerary Highlights

  • Trekking and exploring the Eco-Trail system of Camp John Hay.
  • Visit to the vibrant La Trinidad Strawberry Farm in Benguet.
  • Boat ride and leisure time at Burnham Park.
  • A relaxing evening bonfire experience (seasonal).
